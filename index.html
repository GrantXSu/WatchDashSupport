WatchDashSupport

Help for WatchDash, a fast Apple Watch arcade platformer with tap jumps, ship portals, coins, skins, and level completion.

How To Play

  • Tap to start a level and jump as the cube.
  • In ship mode, press to fly up and release to sink down.
  • Collect coins from harder routes to unlock skins.
  • Finish levels to save completion progress.

Troubleshooting

  • If a tap does not register, restart the app and try again.
  • If progress or coins look wrong, make sure the app was not removed from the watch, since deleting the app can remove local save data.
  • If gameplay feels too small, use a larger Apple Watch display when available.

Privacy

WatchDash does not require an account. The game is designed to run on Apple Watch and uses local game progress such as coins, selected skin, and level completion.
